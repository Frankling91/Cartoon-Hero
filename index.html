Moomintroll

About Moomintroll

Moomintroll is the main character in the Moomin books by Tove Jansson. He is a Moomitroll or Moomin and lives in the Moominhouse together with his father Moominpappa and his mother Moominmamma. He is best friends with Snufkin, and good friends with many others who live in Moominvalley, such as Snorkmaiden and Sniff, and often goes on adventures with them.

“I only want to live in peace and plant potatoes and dream!”

Characteristics

  1. He has white fur
  2. He has a big round nose like a hippo
  3. He is kind but can sometimes be emotional
  4. He tends to worry a lot
